Our Story

Discover the passion and tradition behind every cup of Chai Bliss

The Beginning

Founded in 2015 by tea enthusiast Priya Sharma, Chai Bliss began as a small family business with a simple mission: to share the authentic flavors and rich traditions of tea culture with the world.

What started as a weekend farmers market stall has grown into a beloved tea company, serving thousands of customers who appreciate quality, authenticity, and the perfect cup of chai.

Today, we continue to honor traditional tea-making methods while embracing innovation to bring you the finest tea experience possible.

Our Mission

To create meaningful connections through the timeless ritual of tea, bringing people together one cup at a time.

Sustainability

We partner with ethical tea gardens that prioritize environmental conservation and fair trade practices.

Quality

Every tea leaf is carefully selected and tested to ensure the highest quality and most authentic flavors.

Community

We believe in building strong relationships with our customers, suppliers, and the communities we serve.
